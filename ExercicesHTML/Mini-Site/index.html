Mars is the fourth planet from the sun and the second-smallest planet in the Solar System.
Its size is approximately 3 389,5 Km.
The "Red Planet" appears in the 2015 film adaptation "Martian" by Director Ridley Scott staring Matt Damon

Jupiter is the fifth planet from the sun and the largest is the Solar System.
Its size is approximately 69 991 Km.
Jupiter appears in the 1968 movie "2001: A Space Odyssey" staring Keir Dullea.

Venus is the second planet from the sun and third smallest planet in the Solar System.
Its size is approximately 6 052 Km.
Venus also appears in the 1968 movie "2001: A Space Odyssey" staring Keir Dullea.
